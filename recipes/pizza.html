Pizza

Description

Authentic tuna pizza that is a staple in Europe. If you haven't heard of this, do yourself a favour and try it.

Indigredients

  • 1 batch of pizza dough
  • 400 ml - 13.5 oz tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • 350 mgs - 12.5 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 2x185mgs - 6.5 oz canned tuna
  • 1 onion thinly sliced
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Let the dough rest, prepare toppings. Shred the mozzarella
  2. Prepare tomate base. Mix tomato puree with extra virgin olive oil, oregano, and salt to taste
  3. When pizza dough is ready, divide into 4 small balls and roll them with a rolling pin. Create 4 circles about 0.5cm think
  4. Spread tomato base on pizza and bake in preheated oven at 180c for 15 minutes
  5. Take them out of the oven, add toppings to pizza. mozzarella, tuna, and sliced onions. Put back into oven for 5 minutes to let cheese melt
  6. When ready, remove pizza from oven and drizzle with extra virgin olive oil
  7. Slap some fresh basil leaves on top and enjoy
